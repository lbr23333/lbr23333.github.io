## Chapter1 Prelimiaries
Def1.(Manifold)
A manifold  $ \mathcal M $ is a Hausdorff topological space $ \{ (u_\alpha,\psi_\alpha)\}_{\alpha\in\Lambda}$ s.t.
* $ \{u_\alpha\} $ is an open cover of M 
* $ \psi_\alpha :u_\alpha $
